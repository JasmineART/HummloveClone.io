Our Story

Building a world where fashion heals, not harms

Founded 2020

It Started With a Simple Question

"Why should looking good require destroying the planet we love?"

In 2020, founders Maya Chen and Alex Rivera were frustrated. They loved fashion, but hated the waste. They cared about style, but not at the cost of exploiting workers or animals. So they made a radical decision: to create a brand that would never compromise on ethics, sustainability, or design.

From a small studio in Portland, HummLove was born—named after the hummingbird, nature's symbol of joy, resilience, and lightness. Like the hummingbird, we believe in making a big impact with a gentle footprint.

Today, we're a certified B Corporation with a community of 50,000+ conscious consumers worldwide, proving that the future of fashion is sustainable, ethical, and beautiful.

Sustainability

Sustainability is at the core of everything we do. From regenerative materials and low-impact dyes to fair wages and transparent sourcing, we continuously measure and improve our environmental and social footprint.

  • 100% plant-based or recycled materials across key collections.
  • Carbon neutral operations and verified offsets for shipments.
  • Fair wages and long-term partnerships with artisan cooperatives.

Our Non-Negotiables

These aren't just values on a website. They're the standards we're measured by every single day.

100% Sustainable Materials

Organic cotton, recycled ocean plastic, plant-based leather, peace silk. Every material is vetted by third-party certifiers and chosen for minimal environmental impact.

  • ✓ GOTS certified organic textiles
  • ✓ FSC certified wood & cork
  • ✓ GRS certified recycled materials

100% Vegan & Cruelty-Free

No leather. No wool. No silk. No animal testing. Ever. We're PETA-approved vegan and prove luxury doesn't require animal exploitation.

  • ✓ PETA-approved vegan certification
  • ✓ Leaping Bunny certified cruelty-free
  • ✓ Plant-based leather alternatives

Fair Trade & Living Wages

Every artisan in our supply chain earns a living wage in safe conditions. 78% of our production partners are women-led cooperatives.

  • ✓ Fair Trade International certified
  • ✓ SA8000 workplace standards
  • ✓ Annual third-party audits

Carbon Neutral Operations

100% carbon neutral since day one. We measure, reduce, and offset all emissions across production, shipping, and operations.

  • ✓ Climate Neutral certified
  • ✓ Renewable energy powered facilities
  • ✓ Plastic-free packaging
